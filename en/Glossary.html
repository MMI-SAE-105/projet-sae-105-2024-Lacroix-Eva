Glossary

Talking Stick

An object traditionally used by Native American tribes to regulate discussions. The person holding the stick had the right to speak, while others had to listen in silence.

Mute Button

A function in digital environments that allows temporarily muting the sound during meetings or video conferences. Used to manage online exchanges.

Public Communication

The process by which an individual or organization addresses a large audience, often through media such as radio, television, or social networks.

Digital Surveillance

This involves monitoring and collecting individuals' online activities, communications, and data by governments, companies, or other entities.
